GeoNames (https://www.geonames.org/)

The bundled "cities15000" dataset (assets/cities15000.tsv) is a trimmed extract
of the GeoNames geographical database, used for fully on-device reverse geocoding
(coordinates to a coarse "City, Country" label). Only the city name, latitude,
longitude and ISO country code columns are retained.
